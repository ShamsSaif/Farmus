ABOUT US

We are an agro-based company that provides its customers with fresh agro-products in a subscription format. Our company is currently in the ‘bricks and clicks’ format which will eventually change to ‘bricks and mortars’. This company follows both B2B and B2C models of business by allowing customers to invest in the production of their daily groceries. These subscriptions allow consumers to save upto 30% on their food costs and also earn some profit in the process.
